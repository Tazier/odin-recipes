Spaghetti Pomodoro

Ingredients

  • 8 oz spaghetti noodles
  • 2 cups of tomato sauce
  • 2 whole tomatoes
  • parsley to taste
  • rosemary to taste
  • thyme to taste

Steps

  1. Boil the spaghetti noodles for 8 to 15 minutes.
  2. Chop the tomatoes into small one inch cubes.
  3. open the cans of tomato sauce
  4. drain spaghetti noodles using colander or by carefully pouring all the water out using the spoon to keep from losing any noodles
  5. put spaghetti noodles in bowl and cover with tomato sauce and tomatoes
  6. add parsley,rosemary and thyme to food let sit for a couple minutes and enjoy
