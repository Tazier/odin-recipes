I love this Kale salad and this is a staple in my household great nutrients and very tasty

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of kale
  • Lemon Juice and salt
  • Pine nuts or Shelled sunflower seed
  • Oil and vinegar dressing
  • 1 to 2 cloves of garlic adjust for taste
  • Shredded cheese
  • berries or other fruit you would like to taste

Steps

  1. Take your bowl and fill with enough kale that its sitting over the bowl a bit
  2. Generously add lemon juice and salt you only need one but both of them are great
  3. add garlic after crushing and getting that normal garlic smell
  4. Wash hands then start vigorously crushing all your kale by hand the salt and lemon juice will help this process and make the leaves look small and even watery if you omitted the lemon juice
  5. wash the remaining kale off your hands and taste a piece to make sure most of the bitterness is gone
  6. add all other ingredients and then mix them all up before adding the dressing
  7. Enjoy try not to make more than you'll eat and next time just repeat the steps you should have plenty leftover to make next time
