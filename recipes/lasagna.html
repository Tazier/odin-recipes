Lasagna Traditional

In this recipe i will teach you how to cook an amazing meal for you and your family

This meal will feed up to 8 people

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes and 2 cans of tomato paste
  • 3/4 cup of grated parmesan cheese
  • 3/4 cup of mozzarella
  • Parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning,fennel seeds and black pepper
  • 1 egg
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 cup of onion and 2 cloves of garlic

Steps to cooking your lasagna

  1. cook ground beef with garlic and onion until browned
  2. stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce,tomato paste, and water. Season it with sugar, parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds and pepper
  3. boil lasagna noodles for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water.
  4. in a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining 2 tablespoons of parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  5. preheat oven to 375 degrees
  6. to assemble, spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x12 inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce. Spread with 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture.Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce over the mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup of parmesan cheese. Repeat until your out of all the fixings
  7. Put in oven for 25 minutes with foil on it. Take out and remove foil put it back in for 25 minutes
  8. Let rest for 15 minutes and serve.
